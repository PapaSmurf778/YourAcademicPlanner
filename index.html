Who Are We?

We are a team of driven individuals who understand the challenges that students face when balancing academics, extracurriculars, and personal life. We created YAP with the intention to simplify students' lives, giving them a tool that not only organizes their schedules but also motivates them to excel in all areas of life. YAP is designed to provide students with the resources and structure they need to stay on top of their coursework while freeing up time for their passions and goals.

Why People Should Choose Us

YAP isn’t just another planner—it's your personal academic assistant, built to help you succeed. We go beyond basic scheduling by offering:

  • Customizability: Tailor the planner to fit your exact needs, whether you're balancing multiple extracurriculars, working part-time, or simply managing a heavy class load.
  • Automation: The automatic grade calculator and assignment tracker help you stay on top of your coursework without constantly worrying about deadlines and grades.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Our intuitive design ensures that using the planner is both easy and enjoyable, minimizing time spent on administration and maximizing your productivity.
  • Real-Time Updates: Stay updated on assignments, deadlines, and grades, allowing you to plan ahead and make adjustments as needed.
  • Support: We’re not just offering a product; we’re offering a partnership. Our team is dedicated to helping you optimize your planner for maximum productivity and success.
